## Ingredients
1. Whole eggs – 2
Calories: 140
Protein: 12 g

2. Egg whites – 150 g (~4–5 whites)
Calories: 75
Protein: 16 g

3. Chickpeas (cooked) – 80 g
Calories: 130
Protein: 7 g

4. Low-fat cottage cheese – 100 g
Calories: 80
Protein: 11 g

5. Spinach – 1.5 cups
Calories: 10
Protein: 1.5 g

6. Bell pepper – 70 g
Calories: 20
Protein: 1 g

7. Onion – 40 g
Calories: 16
Protein: 0.5 g

8. Olive oil – 1 tsp
Calories: 40
Protein: 0 g

9. Spices
Paprika, cumin, chili flakes, garlic powder, salt, black pepper

Very high protein for a vegetarian meal.

## Instructions
Instructions
1. Cook the vegetables

Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a pan.
Add onion and bell pepper and sauté for about 3 minutes.

2. Add chickpeas

Add the chickpeas, paprika, cumin, and garlic powder.
Cook for 2–3 minutes so they get slightly toasted.

3. Add eggs

Whisk together:

2 whole eggs

150 g egg whites

Pour into the pan and scramble slowly.

4. Add spinach

Add spinach and cook until wilted.

5. Finish with cottage cheese

Mix in cottage cheese at the end so it becomes creamy and slightly melted.

Why this meal works

• Eggs + egg whites → complete protein
• Chickpeas → fiber + slow carbs
• Cottage cheese → extra protein + creaminess

It’s very filling, which helps when you’re trying to stay in a calorie deficit.
