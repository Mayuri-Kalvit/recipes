## Instructions
1. Cook pasta

Cook 75 g pasta in salted boiling water until al dente.
Reserve ¼ cup pasta water before draining.

2. Make the high-protein sauce

Blend together:

120 g tofu

100 g cottage cheese

garlic

nutritional yeast

lemon juice

salt + pepper

2–3 tbsp pasta water

Blend until completely smooth and creamy.

3. Build the pasta

Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a pan.

Add cherry tomatoes and cook 2 minutes.

Add spinach until wilted.

Pour in the tofu-cottage sauce.

Add cooked pasta and mix well.

Add a splash of pasta water to loosen the sauce.

Cook for 1–2 minutes until creamy.
