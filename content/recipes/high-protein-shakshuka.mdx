## Ingredients

Calories: 40

Protein: 2g

5. Onion – 50 g

Calories: 20

Protein: 0.5g

6. Bell pepper – 70 g

Calories: 20

Protein: 1g

7. Garlic – 2 cloves

Calories: 8

Protein: 0.4g

8. Olive oil – 1 tsp

Calories: 40

Protein: 0g

Calories: 7

Protein: 1g

Paprika

Salt & pepper

(Negligible calories)

## Instructions
Instructions

Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a pan.

Add onions and garlic, sauté for 2–3 minutes.

Add bell pepper and cook another 2 minutes.

Pour in crushed tomatoes and add paprika, cumin, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.

Let the sauce simmer for 5–6 minutes until slightly thick.

Add spinach and stir until wilted.

Make small wells in the sauce and crack 2 whole eggs into them.

Pour the egg whites gently around the pan.

Add spoonfuls of cottage cheese into the sauce.

Cover and cook 4–5 minutes until eggs are just set.

Finish with fresh parsley or cilantro.
