## Ingredients
A quick and healthy vegan stir fry with crispy tofu and vegetables.


- 1 block firm tofu, pressed and cubed
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sriracha
- 1 tsp sesame oil

## Instructions
1. Heat sesame oil in a wok over high heat.
2. Fry tofu until golden and crispy on all sides.
3. Add vegetables and stir fry for 3-4 minutes.
4. Pour in soy sauce and sriracha, toss well.
5. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
