## Ingredients
- 170g Greek Yoghurt
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 cup mixed berries
- 1 tbsp soaked chia seeds
- 2 tbsp low fat granola (optional)

## Instructions
1. Place Greek yoghurt in a bowl.
2. Top with berries, chia seeds, and granola.
3. Drizzle with honey and enjoy!
