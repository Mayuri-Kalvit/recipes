## Ingredients
1 scoop Naked Whey protein powder (30 g)
Calories: 120
Protein: 25 g

Greek yogurt (non-fat) – 170 g (~¾ cup)
Calories: 100
Protein: 17 g

Blueberries – 100 g (~¾ cup)
Calories: 57
Protein: 0.7 g

Unsweetened almond milk – 240 ml (1 cup)
Calories: 30
Protein: 1 g

Chia seeds – 1 tsp (optional)
Calories: 20
Protein: 1 g

Ice cubes – 4–5
Calories: 0
Protein: 0 g

## Instructions
Add almond milk to a blender first.

Add Greek yogurt and protein powder.

Add blueberries and chia seeds.

Add ice cubes.

Blend for 20–30 seconds until smooth.

Tips to Make It Even Better

Thicker smoothie:
Use frozen blueberries instead of fresh.

More protein (post-run version):
Add 100 g extra Greek yogurt
→ +60 calories, +10 g protein.

Creamier texture:
Add ¼ frozen banana.
