## Ingredients
1. Whole eggs – 2
Calories: 140
Protein: 12 g

2. Egg whites – 150 g (~4–5 whites)
Calories: 75
Protein: 16 g

3. Cooked lentils – 120 g
Calories: 140
Protein: 10 g

4. Crushed tomatoes – 200 g
Calories: 40
Protein: 2 g

5. Onion – 50 g
Calories: 20
Protein: 0.5 g

6. Bell pepper – 80 g
Calories: 24
Protein: 1 g

7. Garlic – 2 cloves
Calories: 8
Protein: 0.4 g

8. Spinach – 1 cup
Calories: 7
Protein: 1 g

9. Olive oil – 1 tsp
Calories: 40
Protein: 0 g

10. Spices
Paprika, cumin, chili flakes, black pepper, salt

## Instructions
1. Build the base

Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a skillet.
Add onion, garlic, and bell pepper and cook for 3–4 minutes.

2. Add the sauce

Pour in crushed tomatoes and spices.
Let it simmer 5 minutes until slightly thick.

3. Add lentils

Stir in cooked lentils and spinach.
Cook another 2 minutes.

4. Add eggs

Make small wells in the sauce and crack 2 whole eggs into them.
Pour the egg whites around the pan.

5. Cook

Cover and cook 4–5 minutes until eggs are just set.
