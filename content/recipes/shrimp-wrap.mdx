## Ingredients
1 Lavash Flatbread - 180cals,	10g protein
8 Meatballs (Trader Joes, Plain)	- 300cals, 32g protein
100g Butternut Squash (Trader Joes, Zigzags) - 45cals
1 Low Fat Cheese Slice - (70cals, optional)
2tbsp mix of Sour cream + schezwan chutney + sweet chilli sauce - 100cals

## Instructions
- Bake Butternut Squash with a seasoning of choice (I usually just do salt, pepper and paprika) for 20mins at 420F
- On a pan, heat up 8 meatballs until throughly cooked
- Heat up a piece of lavash flatbread in microwave for 45s
- Lay a piece of cheese slice on it (optional) and heat again for 30s
- In a bowl mix 1tbsp of sour cream, schezwan chutney, sweet chill sauce each
- Spread it on the flatbread and lay meatballs and butternut squash on it
- Wrap and enjoy!
