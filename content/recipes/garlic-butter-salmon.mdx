## Ingredients
Tender salmon fillets cooked in a rich garlic butter sauce.


- 1 salmon fillet
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Lemon wedges for serving

## Instructions
1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
3. Place salmon in the skillet skin-side down.
4. Cook for 4-5 minutes, then flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
5. Garnish with parsley and serve with lemon.
