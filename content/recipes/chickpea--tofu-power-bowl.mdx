## Ingredients
1. Firm tofu – 150 g

Calories: 120

Protein: 15 g

2. Chickpeas (cooked) – 100 g

Calories: 164

Protein: 9 g

3. Spinach – 2 cups

Calories: 14

Protein: 2 g

4. Bell pepper – 80 g

Calories: 24

Protein: 1 g

5. Cucumber – 100 g

Calories: 15

Protein: 1 g

6. Nutritional yeast – 1 tbsp

Calories: 20

Protein: 3 g

7. Tahini – 1 tsp

Calories: 30

Protein: 1 g

8. Lemon juice – 1 tbsp

Calories: 4

Protein: 0 g

9. Olive oil – 1 tsp

Calories: 40

Protein: 0 g

10. Spices

Paprika

Cumin

Garlic powder

Salt & pepper

## Instructions
1. Cook the tofu

Cut tofu into cubes.

Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a pan.

Add tofu with paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper.

Cook 6–7 minutes until slightly crispy.

2. Warm the chickpeas

Add chickpeas to the same pan and sauté for 2–3 minutes with garlic powder and paprika.

3. Make the dressing

Mix together:

tahini

lemon juice

1–2 tbsp water

pinch salt

This makes a light creamy sauce.

4. Assemble the bowl

In a bowl add:

spinach

bell peppers

cucumber

tofu

chickpeas

Drizzle with tahini dressing and sprinkle nutritional yeast on top.
