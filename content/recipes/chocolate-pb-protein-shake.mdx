## Ingredients
1 scoop Naked Whey protein powder (30 g)
Calories: 120
Protein: 25 g

Non-fat Greek yogurt – 150 g
Calories: 90
Protein: 15 g

Unsweetened cocoa powder – 1 tbsp
Calories: 12
Protein: 1 g

Powdered peanut butter (PB2) – 2 tbsp
Calories: 60
Protein: 6 g

Unsweetened almond milk – 1 cup (240 ml)
Calories: 30
Protein: 1 g

## Instructions
Add almond milk to the blender first.

Add Greek yogurt and protein powder.

Add cocoa powder and powdered peanut butter.

Add ice cubes.

Blend for 20–30 seconds until smooth and thick.

Optional upgrades

More carbs for post-run recovery

Add ½ banana
+50 calories
+1 g protein

Thicker milkshake texture

Add ½ cup frozen cauliflower (sounds weird, tastes neutral but makes it super creamy).
