## Ingredients
1. Cooked lentils – 150 g

Calories: 175

Protein: 13 g

2. Low-fat cottage cheese – 150 g

Calories: 120

Protein: 17 g

3. Spinach – 2 cups

Calories: 14

Protein: 2 g

4. Cherry tomatoes – 100 g

Calories: 18

Protein: 1 g

5. Cucumber – 100 g

Calories: 15

Protein: 1 g

6. Red onion – 40 g

Calories: 16

Protein: 0.5 g

7. Olive oil – 1 tsp

Calories: 40

Protein: 0 g

8. Lemon juice – 1 tbsp

Calories: 4

Protein: 0 g

9. Spices

Black pepper

Chili flakes

Roasted cumin powder

Salt

## Instructions
1. Prepare the lentils

Warm the cooked lentils in a pan with a pinch of cumin, salt, and chili flakes for 2–3 minutes.

2. Prepare the vegetables

In a bowl add:

spinach

cucumber

cherry tomatoes

sliced red onion

3. Assemble the bowl

Add the warm lentils on top of the vegetables.

4. Add the protein

Add 150 g cottage cheese on top.

5. Make the dressing

Mix olive oil + lemon juice + black pepper + pinch salt, and drizzle over the bowl.
