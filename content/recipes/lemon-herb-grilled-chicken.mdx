## Ingredients
A simple and delicious grilled chicken recipe with fresh herbs and lemon.


- 2 chicken breasts
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken with lemon juice, garlic, oil, and herbs for 15 minutes.
2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
3. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through.
4. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
