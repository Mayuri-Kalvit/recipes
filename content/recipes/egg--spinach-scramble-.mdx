## Ingredients
1. Whole eggs – 2

Calories: 140

Protein: 12g

2. Egg whites – 200 g (about 6 whites)

Calories: 100

Protein: 22g

3. Low-fat cottage cheese – 80 g

Calories: 65

Protein: 9g

4. Spinach – 2 cups

Calories: 14

Protein: 2g

5. Mushrooms – 100 g

Calories: 22

Protein: 3g

6. Cherry tomatoes – 80 g

Calories: 15

Protein: 1g

7. Onion – 40 g

Calories: 16

Protein: 0.5g

8. Olive oil – 1 tsp

Calories: 40

Protein: 0g

9. Spices

Black pepper

Garlic powder

Chili flakes

Salt

(negligible calories)

## Instructions
Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a non-stick pan.

Add onions and mushrooms, cook for 3–4 minutes.

Add spinach and tomatoes, cook until spinach wilts.

In a bowl whisk together:

2 whole eggs

200 g egg whites

spices

Pour the egg mixture into the pan.

Cook slowly on medium-low heat while stirring gently.

Add cottage cheese and mix until creamy.

Finish with black pepper and chili flakes.
